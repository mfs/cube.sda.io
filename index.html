Mike's 3x3 Algorithms

For the last year or so I have been playing with speedcubes and learning how to solve them. This is a list of the last layer algorithms I know, or am learning. Mainly placing them here so I can use them as a reference. I'll be adding to them over time. Still need to add my PLL algorithms.

OLL

Cross

Bar

F (R U R' U') F'

L

f (R U R' U') f'

Dot

F (R U R' U') F' f (R U R' U') f'

Corners

Sune

R U R' U R U2 R'

Antisune

L' U' L U' L' U2 L

I do Antisune as a mirrored Sune using my left hand. Not the fastest way to do it but easy to remember.

Pi
